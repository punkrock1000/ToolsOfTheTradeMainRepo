# ToolsOfTheTrade

To play Tools of the Trade:
1- Go here to download the executable's folder (zipped): https://drive.google.com/file/d/10_1FRQ4W0y9g0RhbgkNPxZIBOnytNWu3/view?usp=sharing
2- Once downloaded, extract its content
3- Click on the extracted "Tools of the Trade Game" folder to find the game.
4- Launch the game simply by clicking on the executable file called "TheToolsOfTheTradeLauncher.exe"

The game is playable with mouse and keyboard,
however it is recommended to play it using a Xbox One or Xbox 360 controller
(or even a PS3/PS4 controller set up to mimic the 360 controller)
